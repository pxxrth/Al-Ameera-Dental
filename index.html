Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our friendly team.

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 9 AM to 9 PM, Saturday to Thursday. We are closed on Fridays.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a wide range of major insurance providers. Please contact us with your insurance details to confirm coverage.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

For dental emergencies, please call us directly. We offer same-day appointments for urgent cases to provide immediate relief and care.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and approved insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment plans for certain treatments.
